About

Dedicated to Creating Champions in Life

Who are we?

Welcome to Bay Area Equestrian Center (BAEC), a full service equestrian facility, riding academy, and national caliber Arabian show resource.

We are conveniently located on 14 acres in Pearland, Texas where we have been a vital part of the Houston equestrian community since 1995.

Many of our students go on to compete in show programs, earning scholarships and other numerous awards.

What we do

Whether you are interested in our riding academy, horse camp, competitive show program or boarding services; we welcome all ages the ability to learn more about our facility and what BAEC has to offer you.
